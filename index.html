Introduction

Anish Agrawal (born 24 September 1992), better known as Anish Agrawal, is a student at Washington University in St. Louis. Graduated with a Applied physics degree from Providence College in 2014, Anish moved to St. Louis to pursue an engineering degree in the field of Mechanical and Systems enginnering. Anish aspires to be en entrepreneur and solve real world problems through his knowlege of engineering combined with his problem solving skills.

Early Childhood

Anish was born in Lahan, Sagarmatha, Nepal in 1992 to Rekha and Naresh Agrawal.He spent his childhood in Lahan. His father was a small business owner and managed a distributing outlet for different products. Anish grew up in a two-room apartment on Lahan-1, Mahendrachowk, Nepal. Anish completed his Class X at Little Star School in Lahan and completed the Class XII Delhi Public School R.K.Puram located in New Delhi, India.
